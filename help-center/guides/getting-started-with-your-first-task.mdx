---
title: Getting Started with Your First Task
description: Complete your first feedback task on Feedquire.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up or Log In">
    Register at feedquire.com or log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Available Tasks">
    Visit your dashboard to see current available AI feedback tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the Task">
    Click on a task and follow instructions provided. Answer all questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Feedback">
    Once finished, click "Submit" to send your feedback for approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>
