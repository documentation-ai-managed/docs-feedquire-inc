## Overview

Complete AI feedback tasks to earn up to $14 per task.

## Payment Process

1. Submit your completed feedback via the dashboard.
2. Wait for task review and approval.
3. Payouts are processed every Friday.

## Payment Methods

- PayPal
- Bank transfer (selected regions)

## Minimum Withdrawal

You need at least $10 in your account to withdraw.
