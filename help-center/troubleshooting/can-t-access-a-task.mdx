---
title: Can't Access a Task
description: What to do if you can't open assigned tasks.
---

## Symptoms

- Task won't load after clicking
- "Task unavailable" error

## Causes

- Task slot limit reached
- Network connectivity issues

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check task status">
    Refresh the dashboard. The task might be full or expired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your internet">
    Ensure you have a stable connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If problem persists, email support@feedquire.com with the Task ID.
  </Step>
</Steps>
