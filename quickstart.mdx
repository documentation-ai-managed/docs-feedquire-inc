## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you meet these requirements to join Feedquire's global community of AI testers.

<Callout kind="info">

- You must be at least 18 years old
- Have a valid email address for verification
- Prepare basic profile information (name, location, payment method like PayPal)
- Stable internet connection for task completion

</Callout>

## Create Your Feedquire Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes to access paid AI testing opportunities.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Signup Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to the [Feedquire signup page](https://app.feedquire.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Inbox" icon="mail">
    Confirm your account by clicking the verification link sent to your email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify Your Profile

Complete profile verification to unlock all testing tasks and ensure smooth payouts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload ID" icon="upload">
    Go to your profile settings at `https://app.feedquire.com/profile` and upload a government-issued ID for age and identity verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Payment Info" icon="credit-card">
    Link a PayPal account or bank details under the Payments tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Quiz" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a short qualification quiz about AI testing best practices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Verification typically takes 24 hours. You'll receive an email notification when approved.
</Callout>

## Browse and Complete Your First Task

Once verified, explore available tasks paying up to `$14` each.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser Tasks" icon="globe">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Browse Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
        Log in at `https://app.feedquire.com/dashboard` and filter tasks by "Beginner" level.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Task" icon="play-circle">
        Choose a task like "Evaluate Chatbot Responses" and click "Start".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Provide Feedback" icon="edit-3">
        Follow instructions, test the AI, and submit detailed feedback with screenshots.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App Tasks" icon="smartphone">
    Download the Feedquire tester app from your app store, log in, and follow similar steps for on-device AI testing.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Track Earnings and Payouts

Monitor your progress and withdraw earnings easily.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Earnings Dashboard" icon="bar-chart-3" href="https://app.feedquire.com/earnings" target="_blank">
    View task history, pending reviews, and total earnings in real-time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Request Payout" icon="dollar-sign" href="https://app.feedquire.com/payouts">
    Withdraw to PayPal once you reach the `$10` minimum threshold.
  </Card>
</Columns>

To integrate earnings tracking into your own app, use this simple API example:

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.feedquire.com/v1/earnings?userId=YOUR_USER_ID', {
    headers: {
      'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_TOKEN'
    }
  });
  const data = await response.json();
  console.log(`Total earned: ${data.total}`);
  ```
  ```python
  import requests
  response = requests.get(
    'https://api.feedquire.com/v1/earnings?userId=YOUR_USER_ID',
    headers={'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_TOKEN'}
  )
  data = response.json()
  print(f"Total earned: {data['total']}")
  ```
</CodeGroup>
